Provel software foundation was founded on April 10th, 2013. Created with the soul purpose of being a site to share our projects with the world. Our founders are Nate Magee, Nate Morrow and Thomas Hanson.




Our Goals

1- We strive to build products that extend human potential. Things that have or may in the future, change the world drastically. We build products involving technologies in social networking, calculating, etc.

2- We believe as well as many others that the internet should be free, open-source and available to anyone. Which is why the SDK's, IDE's, etc. we use, will always be open-source.
